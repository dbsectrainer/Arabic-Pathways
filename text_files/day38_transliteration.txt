
Shopping Dialogue 1
-------------------
az-zabun: kam si'r hadha al-qamis?
al-ba'i': mi'atan dirham
az-zabun: ghali jiddan, hal yumkin takhfid as-si'r?
al-ba'i': hasanan, mi'a wa thamanun dirham
az-zabun: hasanan, sa'akhudhuhu

Shopping Dialogue 2
-------------------
az-zabun: ayna ghurfat al-qiyas?
al-ba'i': hunak, 'ala al-yamin
az-zabun: hal ladaykum maqas akbar?
al-ba'i': da'ni atahaqqaq. na'am, hadha maqas XL
az-zabun: shukran, sa'ujarribuh


Travel Dialogue 1
-----------------
as-sa'ih: 'afwan, kayfa asil ila burj khalifa?
al-muwatin: yumkinuk istikhdam al-metro ila mahattat burj khalifa/dubai mall
as-sa'ih: kam min al-waqt yastaghrik?
al-muwatin: hawali 'ishrin daqiqa
as-sa'ih: shukran jazilan 'ala al-musa'ada

Travel Dialogue 2
-----------------
as-sa'ih: mata yughlik al-mathaf?
al-muwadhdhaf: nughlik fi as-sa'a ath-thamina masa'an
as-sa'ih: kam rusum ad-dukhul?
al-muwadhdhaf: khamsun dirhaman lil-kibar, wa lit-tullab an-nisf
as-sa'ih: ana talib, hadhihi bitaqati al-jami'iyya


Restaurant Dialogue 1
---------------------
an-nadil: ahlan wa sahlan, kam shakhs?
az-zabun: shakhsan, min fadlik
an-nadil: tafaddalu ma'i
az-zabun: hal ladaykum qa'imat at-ta'am?
an-nadil: tafaddal, khudh waqtak

Restaurant Dialogue 2
---------------------
az-zabun: urid an atlub
an-nadil: madha tuhibb an tatlub?
az-zabun: urid shawarma dajaj wa salata
an-nadil: hasanan, hal turid shay'an akhar?
az-zabun: na'am, kub 'asir burtuqal
